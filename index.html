Lamb Roast

Lamb Roast is a timer App that was build for the Meat & Livestock Association Aus, it was the first App I worked on that was released to the App Store, and a bit of of a challenge illustrating the icons as I'm a vegetarian but I was happy with the result in the end! I also then got the opportunity to learn WatchKit for the Apple Watch App version. Unfortunately it's no longer on the App Store as it has evolved into RoastMate.

SteakMate Japan

This was a super fun project, MLA contacted CLD to do a Japanese version of SteakMate to promote Aussie beef in Japan and I had complete freedom with the design. They wanted a 'Kawaii Australian' Theme so I came up with a Koala riding a piece of steak. I got to create all the animations as well!
