About me

I’m a Designer / web dev who lives in London. Currently I'm working at Airtasker as a Designer and Dev on the UK launch team. With my 5 years of experience - I’ve had the opportunity to learn how to create beautiful and practical experiences, with design and languages such as stylus, pug, and react. I also enjoy working with animation and am super interested in making interactive projects! In my spare time, I create music and watch terrible tv shows on netflix like a regular human.
